690/* macro to define a function that overloads over a GROUP of types -- will declare all the
691 * functions that overload on a Target::type argument and a 'generic' overload that calls
692 * the right specific overload based on options.target
693 * GROUP can be one of
694 * TARGET -- overload on all the different targets
695 * REGISTER_SET -- overload just on the register sets (targets that share a register
696 * set will only have one overload)
697 * TARGET_CLASS -- overload based on the CLASS
698 * RTYPE NAME ARGDECL together make the declaration of the (generic) function, the overloads
699 * will all have a Target::type argument prepended. The final ARGS argument is the argument
700 * list that that will be forwarded (basically ARGDECL without the types)
